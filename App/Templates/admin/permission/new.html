What are Permissions?

Permissions are authorization given to a role. Which can be assigned to one or more users. Permissions can either be individual or as part of a permission group. To where that entire group can be assigned to one or more roles.

Every route within the application requires a specific permission. Which your role must have in order to gain access to perform the necessary action.
